Snellen Vision Chart

How to take the test

  • Sit exactly 1 meter (3 feet) away from your screen.
  • Cover your left eye with your hand (do not press on the eye).
  • Read the letters on the chart from top to bottom.
  • Repeat the process for your right eye.
  • Note: This is a basic screening tool and does not replace a professional clinical eye exam.
