Our Story

A Legacy of Care and Excellence

Founded in 1995, Clear Vision Eye Hospital began with a simple mission: to provide world-class eye care with a personal touch. Over the decades, we have grown from a small clinic into a state-of-the-art facility, serving thousands of patients.

We continually invest in the latest medical technologies and recruit top specialists to ensure our patients receive the best possible outcomes.

Our Mission

To enhance the quality of life of our patients by providing comprehensive, ethical, and advanced eye care services.

Our Vision

To be the global leader in innovative eye care, recognized for excellence, compassion, and cutting-edge research.

Milestones

Our Journey

A timeline of our commitment to excellence and innovation in eye care.

1995
Hospital Founded
Started with a single operating theater and two doctors, dedicated to making high-quality eye care accessible.
2005
LASIK Center Opened
Introduced advanced laser vision correction to the region, restoring clear vision for thousands of patients.
2015
Awarded 'Best Eye Hospital'
Recognized nationally for our unparalleled excellence in patient care, surgical outcomes, and infrastructure.
2023
Robotic Surgery Introduced
Implemented state-of-the-art AI-assisted robotic eye surgeries, setting a new standard for maximum precision.
